MISCONCEPTION AND REMEDIATION

  TARGET AUDIENCE

          This module is meant for in-service and preservice teachers as a means to enrich themselves professionally.


AIM OF MODULE

There are a total of four units in the Module. The purposes of the units are to present a crisp but enriched understanding of the phenomenon of misconceptions and its remediation through alternative instruction.


The aims of the module are as follows:

  • Comprehend the phenomenon of misconception among the students.
  • Understand the role of misconception in learning.

  • Develop insight into the theoretical aspect of remediation of misconception.

  • Understand the instructional design presented for remediation of misconception.

STRUCTURE OF MODULE

There are a total of FOUR units in the Module

Each units has the following structure:

  • Introduction

  • Objectives of the Module

  • Content organized into few simple sections supplemented by Examples and Diagrams

  • Self Assessment Ideas

  • Summary

  • Additional Resources
